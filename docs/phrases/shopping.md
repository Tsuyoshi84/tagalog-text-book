# Shopping Phrases

Practical phrases for markets, stores, and shopping interactions in the Philippines.

## Asking About Items

### Tagalog
*Meron ba kayong...?*

### English
Do you have...?

---

### Tagalog
*May available ba?*

### English
Is it available?

---

### Tagalog
*Ano ang meron kayo?*

### English
What do you have?

---

### Tagalog
*Pakita mo nga.*

### English
Show me please.

---

### Tagalog
*Pwede ko bang tingnan?*

### English
Can I look at it?

---

### Tagalog
*Anong kulay ang meron?*

### English
What colors are available?

---

### Tagalog
*Anong size?*

### English
What size?

---

### Tagalog
*May mas malaki ba?*

### English
Do you have a bigger one?

---

### Tagalog
*May mas maliit ba?*

### English
Do you have a smaller one?

::: tip Bargaining Culture
Bargaining is common in markets and small shops but not in malls or fixed-price establishments. Start by offering 30-40% less than the asking price and negotiate from there.
:::
